====== Wire and Tape Recorders ==

Phonograph, gramophone, wire and tape recorders are //analogue// recorders.
  * phonograph and gramophone store sound waves as undulations of grooves
  * wire and tape recorders store sound waves as fluctuations of magnetization of a magnetic material (steel, iron oxide, etc.)

==== Wire recorders ==
  * wire recorder (Telegraphone) invented in 1889 by [[wp>Valdemar_Poulsen|Valdemar Poulsen]]
  * waves recorded on a magnetized, fast-moving, thin steel wire
  * used between 1920s and 1960s (mainly in North America)

==== Tape recorders (reel-to-reel) ==
  * use a tape coated with iron oxide
  * invented in 1928 by [[wp>Fritz_Pfleumer|Fritz Pfleumer]]
  * paper tapes first, later plastic tapes
  * called //magnetophon// in Europe
  * in use from 1940s
